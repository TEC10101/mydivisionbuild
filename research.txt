- Image masks are found in rogue\sdf\pc\data\decrypted\rogue\baked\art\[ui]\[loose_images]\generated
- naming convention is :<unknown>_<gender(m|f)>_<unknown>_<tier>_<rarity([c]ommon,[u]ncommon,[r]are,[e]pic,[l]egendary)_icon.dds
